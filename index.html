About the Locket

the story of how it all began.

The app's creator,

Matt Moss, developed it as a gift for his girlfriend, allowing them to share real-time photos directly on each other's home screens.

The idea behind

Locket Widget was to create a more natural and effortless way for friends and family to stay connected in real time.

With no likes, filters, or excessive notifications, Locket became a light and fun way to stay connected with close friends. Today, millions of users worldwide use Locket to share small everyday moments, making their home screens more lively and meaningful.
